I am writing to express my strong interest in the Deputy (or Academic) Editor position at the \textit{International Journal of Remote Sensing}. My extensive background in academic publishing, coupled with a robust research and leadership record, aligns well with the requirements for this pivotal role. I am eager to contribute to the journal's continued success and uphold its reputation for publishing cutting-edge research.

% \section*{Editorial and Peer Review Acumen}

My commitment to scholarly integrity is demonstrated through active involvement in the editorial process. I serve on the Editorial Board of the \textit{International Journal of Industrial and Manufacturing Systems Engineering}, where I contribute to strategic direction and uphold rigorous standards. Furthermore, I have completed over 120 peer reviews for more than 30 academic journals, showcasing my efficiency, reliability, and deep practical understanding of research quality and constructive feedback across diverse sub-disciplines. This experience ensures my ability to oversee a rigorous peer review process and maintain high standards.

% \section*{Robust Research and Publication Record}

My active contributions to scientific literature provide a crucial author's perspective on the publication lifecycle. My record includes 34 journal papers and 27 conference papers/posters, with numerous works slated for publication or under revision in prestigious journals like \textit{Composites Part B: Engineering}, \textit{Expert Systems with Applications}, and \textit{Journal of Manufacturing Systems} for 2025. My role as first or corresponding author on many publications signifies direct experience with the entire manuscript process, from conceptualization to revision, and a consistent ability to publish high-impact research in journals such as \textit{International Journal of Machine Tools and Manufacture} (IF=14) and \textit{Journal of Manufacturing Systems} (IF=12.3).

% \section*{Leadership, Project Management, and Strategic Vision}

My experience leading and managing complex research projects directly translates to the operational and strategic demands of a Deputy Editor. I have served as a "Proposal contributor" and "Lead researcher" on multi-million Euro projects, including AutoCAM\_SBO (~2.75M Euros) and MUSIC\_SBO (~3.5M Euros). These roles highlight my skills in project conceptualization, funding acquisition, resource allocation, and team coordination. My contributions to projects like the "National Natural Science Foundation of China" and "CTO Action" , including "Work plan generation based on the product specific preference and the environmental constrains by innovative algorithmic approaches" , demonstrate a forward-thinking mindset applicable to modernizing journal processes. 

% \section*{Technical Expertise and Contribution to Journal's Scope}

My research fields and technical skills are highly relevant to enriching the journal's content. My expertise spans cutting-edge areas such as "Advanced CAD-to-CAM workplan generation and optimization," "Multiphysical Modeling," "In-process Monitoring and Control (based on vision/acoustic data)," and various "Additive Manufacturing (LPBF/WAAM/FFF) and Subtractive Manufacturing (ECM/EDM)" processes. My joint appointments at KU Leuven's Mechanical Engineering and Computer Science departments  highlight my interdisciplinary approach, valuable for evaluating diverse submissions. Furthermore, my development of practical applications like a "Web application of real-time ECM processing profile predictor"  showcases a problem-solving orientation beneficial for improving journal workflows and digital features.

My comprehensive experience on an editorial board, extensive peer review contributions, robust publication record in high-impact journals, and proven leadership in complex research projects make me a strong candidate for the Deputy Editor position. My deep technical expertise in advanced manufacturing and computational methods, combined with a thorough understanding of the academic publishing ecosystem, positions me to significantly contribute to your journal's continued success.

I am eager to discuss my qualifications further. Thank you for your time and consideration.
