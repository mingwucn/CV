\section{Research Vision}
\textbf{Grounded Causal Intelligence} in Manufacturing Research focuses on the synthesis of fundamental manufacturing physics and advanced artificial intelligence to transition from reactive qualification to proactive, "first-time-right" manufacturing. A dual-expertise approach is utilized, combining \textbf{Causal AI} with \textbf{Adaptive Digital Twins} to resolve the "physical residual", which is the gap between idealized simulations and stochastic reality. The primary objective is the development of a "Symbiotic Co-Learning Loop," where AI agents autonomously design minimal physical experiments to correct physics-based models. Applications focus on zero-shot planning for Additive Manufacturing and intelligent state detection in Subtractive Processes.
